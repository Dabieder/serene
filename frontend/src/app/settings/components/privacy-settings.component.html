Download

You can download all the data that has been collected about you. If you choose to do so, an archive (.zip) will be created and you will be able to download it once it is ready.

Delete

You can request the deletion of all the personalized data that is stored about you and which is not directly related to the functionality of Serene, such as log data.

This process will remove the personlization from the following data:

  • Your Learning Plans
  • Your Monitorings
  • Your Click Data

Please note that you cannot revert this process, so once your personalized data is deleted, we will not be able to restore it.
