Download

You can download all the data that has been collected about you. If you choose to do so, an archive (.zip) will be created and you will be able to download it once it is ready.

Delete

You can request the deletion of all the personalized data that is stored about you and which is not directly related to the functionality of Serene, such as log data.

Please note that you cannot revert this process, so once your personalized data is deleted, you will no longer receive personalized recommendations.

Contact

You can contact us at any time if you have questions regarding the collection, storage, or processing of your data.
If you want, you can contact us directly via e-mail or use the contact form below.
